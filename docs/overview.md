# Project Overview

## Context

The company requires a centralized system to manage operational activities related to employees, projects, tasks, budgets, KPIs, and SLAs.

Currently, information is distributed across multiple processes and operational activities, making it difficult to monitor workload, resource utilization, service quality, and project performance in a consistent manner.

To support operational management and reporting, a unified platform is needed to consolidate business data and provide visibility into project execution, resource allocation, KPI achievement, SLA compliance, and budget utilization.

---

## Purpose

The purpose of this project is to develop a Resource Management Platform for IT outsourcing operations.

The platform provides capabilities for:

* Employee and resource management
* Project and task management
* KPI and SLA management
* Budget and effort tracking
* Productivity and capacity monitoring
* Reporting and dashboard visualization

The system serves as a centralized platform for managing operational data and supporting management reporting.
